LinkUp – Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

1. Introduction

LinkUp ("we", "us", or "our") is an 18+ dating app that helps adults discover, match with, and message people nearby. We take the privacy of your personal information seriously — dating data is sensitive, and we treat it that way.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have. It reflects the app's current backend-connected behavior.

Contact / Data controller: Žilvinas Malinauskis — lttitanas@gmail.com

2. Age requirement

LinkUp is strictly for users 18 years or older. You must confirm you are 18+ to use the app. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18; if we learn a minor has used LinkUp, we delete their data. See Section 9.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information you provide

  • Phone number — used to create and sign in to your account by one-time SMS code (OTP). It is your account identifier.
  • Name, age, gender — shown on your profile.
  • Bio and interests — free text and tags you choose.
  • Profile photo(s) — selected from your device or taken as a verification selfie. Photos are uploaded to our servers, automatically checked for a real face and disallowed content, and shown to other users.
  • Messages — the content of chats you send to your matches.
  • Reports and blocks — when you report or block another user.

3.2 Information collected automatically

  • Approximate location (city). With your permission, the app reads your device's location to determine your city. Only the city name is sent to and stored on our servers — your precise GPS coordinates are never uploaded or stored; they are used only on your device to look up the city. Location is optional: deny it and LinkUp still works (it shows "Location off").
  • Device identifier. When you sign in, the app sends a device identifier (Android ANDROID_ID) that we use to bind your login session and help prevent fraud and account abuse.
  • Activity within the app. Your swipes, matches, reports, and blocks are stored so the service can function (show you new people, keep matches, enforce blocks).

3.3 Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect payment information (no in-app purchases in this version).
  • We do not use advertising IDs, ad SDKs, or third-party analytics/crash SDKs.
  • We do not access your contacts, calendar, files, microphone, or browsing history.
  • We do not collect or store your precise GPS coordinates on our servers.

5. Permissions We Request

  • Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) — foreground only, to determine your city and help match you with nearby people. We do not request background location. Optional; the app works if you deny it.
  • Camera — LinkUp does not hold a camera permission of its own. When you take a verification selfie, LinkUp opens your device's system camera app to capture the photo. Profile photos can also be chosen with the Android photo picker, which needs no permission.

You can review or revoke permissions any time in Settings → Apps → LinkUp → Permissions.

6. Photos and Verification

Profile and verification photos are uploaded over an encrypted connection and stored on our servers so they can be shown to other users. Each photo is automatically screened (real-face and disallowed-content checks) before your profile is visible. Photos that fail moderation may be rejected. See our content standards for what is and isn't allowed.

7. Who We Share Data With

We do not sell or rent your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing.

8. Data Security and Transfer

  • All traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS); the app disables cleartext connections.
  • Login codes are stored hashed, never in plaintext; session tokens are hashed and rotated.
  • We minimize what we store (e.g. city name, not GPS coordinates) and never log sensitive fields in production.

Our servers may be located outside your country; where they are, we rely on appropriate safeguards for any cross-border transfer.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account, or by emailing lttitanas@gmail.com. Deleting your account removes your profile, photos, swipes, matches, messages, reports, blocks, and sessions from our servers, and wipes local app data. Some records may be retained briefly where required by law or to prevent fraud/abuse, then deleted.

10. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (including under the EU/UK GDPR), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Correct inaccurate data (edit your profile in-app);
  • Delete your data (in-app or by request);
  • Restrict or object to certain processing;
  • Data portability;
  • Withdraw consent (e.g. revoke the location permission) at any time.

To exercise any right, contact lttitanas@gmail.com. You also have the right to complain to your local data-protection authority.

11. Children's Privacy

LinkUp is for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has used LinkUp, email lttitanas@gmail.com and we will delete the account and its data promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will change the "Effective date" above and, for significant changes, notify you in the app. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
